The Higher School of Economics (HSE University) in Moscow is a leading multidisciplinary research university and one of Russia's most prestigious higher education institutions. Known for its rigorous academic standards and modern approach to education, HSE has evolved from a specialized school of economics into a comprehensive university offering top-tier programs in social sciences, humanities, computer science, and engineering. It is highly regarded for its integration into the global academic community, its emphasis on data-driven research, and its strong links to the professional industry. As a key hub for innovation, HSE attracts top talent and consistently ranks among the best universities globally in international rankings such as QS and THE.
