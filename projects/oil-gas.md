## Oil & Gas

Projects in Oil & Gas

### Projects


**[Project 05](/downloads/05.pdf):** Corporate Cashless Payment System (CCPS)

Created a platform for cashless settlement. Reduced fraud by replacing paper fuel coupons with cards. Automated cashless settlements with 70,000 legal entities; reduced reconciliation time 6x, cut fraud losses 10x; received a client commendation.

---

**[Project 06](/downloads/06.pdf):** Loyalty Program

Designed and launched a loyalty system for the fuel retail chain. Implemented a comprehensive loyalty program platform. Participants grew from 1M to 4.5M; marketing effectiveness significantly improved.

---

**[Project 39](/downloads/39.pdf):** Architecture Analysis for Fuel Retail

Resolved loyalty issues, gave future IT landscape improvement plan.

---

**[Project 41](/downloads/41.pdf):** nabled Remuneration Process Transformation
